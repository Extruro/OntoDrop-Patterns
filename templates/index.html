OntoDrop Patterns

OntoDrop identify and extract patterns from a collection of ontologies.

Steps to follow

  1. 1 Prepare the ontology or set of ontologies you want to transform.
  2. 2 They must be inside a .zip file or in .csv.
  3. 3 Perform step 1)Choose options.
  4. 4 You must have both options filled in.
  5. 5 Then go to step 2)Upload your ontologies here.
  6. 6 Attach your file with the ontologies and click Submit.
  7. 7 Wait until Download Patterns button is green.
  8. 8 Now you can download your patterns in a .zip file.
  9. + If you need more information about using the tool, click here.
